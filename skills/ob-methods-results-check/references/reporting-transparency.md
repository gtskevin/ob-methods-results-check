# Reporting Transparency Audit

Check:

1. Sample flow, exclusions, missing values, and cell sizes.
2. Scale anchors, reliability, loadings, CR, AVE, and adaptation notes where relevant.
3. Coefficient type: `b`, `beta`, standardized, or unstandardized.
4. SE, CI, exact `p`, df, effect-size type, and model-comparison baseline.
5. Table notes, significance symbols, variable names, and figure labels.
6. Consistency across abstract, overview, Methods, Results, tables, figures, appendices, and output files.
7. Whether the manuscript distinguishes verified findings from trend-level or exploratory evidence.
