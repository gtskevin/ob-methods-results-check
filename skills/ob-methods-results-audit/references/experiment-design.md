# Experiment Audit

Check:

1. Random assignment, cell sizes, exclusions, stopping rules, power analysis, and preregistration.
2. Manipulation materials against the intended construct and credible alternative explanations.
3. Manipulation checks, their placement, effect size, and demand-characteristic risk.
4. Whether cell means reconstruct marginal means.
5. Whether ANOVA, regression, t-tests, simple effects, degrees of freedom, and effect-size labels are consistent.
6. Whether measured outcomes are behavior, intention, perception, or manipulation checks.
7. Which parts of a mediation chain are experimentally identified.

Do not describe the whole model as causal when only the antecedent or moderator is manipulated.
