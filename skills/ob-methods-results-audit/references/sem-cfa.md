# CFA and SEM Audit

Check:

1. Number of constructs, item counts, indicators, parcels, and reported degrees of freedom.
2. Whether parceling, item deletion, correlated residuals, or post-hoc changes are reported.
3. Alternative models and fit statistics.
4. Estimator, missing-data handling, clustering treatment, and standardization.
5. Construct coverage when controls or adapted measures are also multi-item scales.
6. Whether latent, observed, standardized, and unstandardized coefficients are clearly distinguished.

Use the recalculation helper for conventional correlated-factor CFA degrees of freedom. Treat discrepancies as prompts to inspect the actual model syntax, not as automatic proof of error.
