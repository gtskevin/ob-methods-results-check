# Field Survey Audit

Check:

1. Sample provenance, invitation counts, waves, matching, exclusions, attrition, and missing-data handling.
2. Whether time separation actually separates predictor, mediator, and outcome.
3. Whether same-source measurement remains for key paths.
4. Whether common-method claims are appropriately limited.
5. Whether controls are theoretically justified and whether collinearity or suppression is plausible.
6. Whether variable coding in text matches tables and software output.
7. Whether adapted measures require additional validity evidence.

Do not treat a Harman single-factor test as proving common-method bias is absent.
