# Mediation, Moderation, and Moderated Mediation Audit

Check:

1. Whether the `a`, `b`, `c`, and `c'` paths come from correctly specified equations.
2. Whether the outcome equation includes the antecedent when interpreting a standard mediation `b` path.
3. Whether indirect effects can be reconstructed as `a * b`.
4. Whether CI methods are named correctly: bootstrap, Monte Carlo, or another method.
5. Whether interactions include constituent main effects.
6. Whether continuous moderators are probed at clearly defined values such as `+/- 1 SD`.
7. Whether binary experimental conditions are described as conditions rather than `+/- 1 SD`.
8. Whether simple slopes, plotted values, conditional indirect effects, and text all come from the same model.
9. Whether an index of moderated mediation is distinguished from the high-low conditional indirect-effect difference.
10. Whether an unsupported interaction is incorrectly used as the only test of moderated mediation.
